Lazztech Hub is a tool for bringing communities of people together for in person gatherings.

Hubs use Geo-fences which are a virtual perimeter around the Hub's physical location.

As a Hub member you can see which of the Hub's community members are presently at the Hub's location.

This gives you as a user a glanceable overview of who's present at your favorite community gathering spots.

A Hub is a private invite only virtual community associated with a physical location in the world.

As the admin of a Hub you can deactivate it at any time to conceal the details of which members are presently away or at the location.

You may then at any time Reactivate your Hub and members will recieve a notification that the Hub is active.
