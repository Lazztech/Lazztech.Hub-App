Tutorial

Lazztech Hub is a tool for bringing communities of people together for in person gatherings.

Hubs make use of geo-fences around the coordinates of the Hub's GPS coordinates.

As a Hub member you can see which of the Hub's community members are presently at the Hub's location.

This gives you as a user a high level overview of what's happening with your favorite community gathering spots.

What is a Geo-Fence?

A Geo-Fence is a virtual perimeter around a location that your mobile device can detect in the background when you enter or exit

What is a Hub?

A Hub is a private invite only virtual community tied to a physical location in the world. When you create a Hub the coordinates are registered to that Hub via GPS.

What about privacy?

Hubs are private & invite only which means it's details are only viewable by that Hub's members. No one will be able to see these details without inviting them into the Hub.

Can I turn a Hub on/off?

As the admin of a Hub you can Deactivate it at any time to conceal the details of which members are presently away or at the location.

You may then at any time Reactivate your Hub. This will send a notification to it's members as a que to that the space is open to gatherings.

Let's setup permissions

You'll need to enable some permissions on your device for the app to function properly.

For example you'll be prompted to enable the background geo-location for this app so that the Geo-Fences for your Hubs can function properly.

Push Notification permission

You'll also be prompted for push notifications which are optional but recommended.

These are used for events such as one of the Hubs you're a member of becoming active.

Prompt for permission

Geo-Fence permissions

You'll be prompted for background location permissions. These are used so that your device can detect Hub Geo-Fences and check you in or out of a Hub.

You'll also be prompted for permission to detect when your device is in motion. This is used to conserve battery by enabling your device to only look for Geo-Fences when you're in motion.

Prompt for permission

Verify Permissions

The Lazztech Hub App requires your permissions to be configured properly so that the Geo-Fences can function in the background.

Ensure that following settings are configured properly in your permission settings.

Location is configured to Always

Motion & Fitness is toggled on

Background App Refresh is toggled on
